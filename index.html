The Camping Community Welcomes You!

We Welcome You to Camp Valley!

Welcome to our website! Camp Counselor Sarah is here to inform you of the wonderful time you will have here at our new camp site! Camp Valley offers a haven and a nice getaway for children and adults of all ages. Here at Camp Valley, we invite you to kick back, relax and enjoy the wonderful events and scenery that nature has to offer. So, to the people all around, come on down to the best new camp around: Camp Valley! We hope to see you soon.

Our Aim

Our goal is a simple one, but we aim to be the escape that most people would want. We know times are hard and everyone is going through something. We aim to be your escape from reality. Here at Camp Valley, you can just kick back and relax. You do not have to worry about the real world We aim to be some sort of remedy for your mental and physical health.
