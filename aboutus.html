Learn About Us

Camp Valley Community is a campsite that operates in the summer and fall. Camp Valley was founded on the beliefs that everyone should have a safe space to run to every once and a while. That is what we do. We give our campers a safe space to let loose and relax.

Camp Valley operates for two months in Summer and one month in the Fall. We have three locations, one in Henry , Griffin, and Clayton County. You can visit any of the three locations and receive benefits. We have a wonderful staff of camp counselors who would be happy to assist you. We are also hiring new counselors.
